HEFC 2026

Hydrogen Energy & Fuel Cell Conference 2026 will be held at the International Building, National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, on October 2-3, 2026.

Date
October 2-3, 2026
Venue
International Building (IB), NTUST
Organizer
Department of Materials Science and Engineering, NTUST

About

HEFC 2026 brings together researchers, industry professionals, and technical experts in hydrogen energy and fuel cell technologies.

Conference

Hydrogen Energy & Fuel Cell Conference 2026

The conference emphasizes academic exchange, technology transfer, and international collaboration.

Co-organizers

Taiwan Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Association

Co-organized with the College of Engineering, NTUST.

Chair

Prof. Cheng-Hao Wang

Conference chair and head of the Energy Materials Laboratory.

Agenda Snapshot

Day 1 is the technology forum and Day 2 is the academic conference.

Day 1｜Technology Forum

Forum sessions on infrastructure, applications, supply chain, policy, and industry outlook.

Day 2｜Academic Conference

Opening ceremony, keynote talks, oral sessions, poster presentations, and award ceremony.
